
Replication files for: "Personal Values or a Democratic Value? Revisiting Public Reactions to the Failure of Civilian Control in Japan"

(Last update: February 19, 2026)

Files in this repository: 

* <code>appendix_g1_20260208.pdf</code>: Online Appendix of the paper.
* <code>dataset_g1_main_cleaned.rds</code>: Experiment dataset.
* <code>codebook_g1_main.txt</code> Codebook for the dataset.
* <code>analysis_g1_main_v2.R</code> Codes to execute the main analysis. Export Tables 2, 3 and Figures 1, 2 in the main text as well as Figures A.1 and A.2 in the Online Appendix. 
* <code>analysis_g1_controlled_v2.1.R</code> Codes to execute the analysis with covariates. Export Tables C.1, C.2 and Figures C.1, C.2 in the Online Appendix. 
* <code>analysis_g1_withpredv_v2.R</code> Codes to execute the analysis with pretreatment trust in the JSDF as a covariate. Export Tables D.1, D.2 and Figures D.1, D.2 in the Online Appendix.
* <code>analysis_g1_main_nosatisficer_v2.R</code> Codes to execute the main analysis. Export Tables E.1, E.2 and Figures E.1, E.2 in the main text as well as Figures A.1 and A.2 in the Online Appendix. 
* <code>analysis_g1_controlled_nosatisficer_v2.R</code> Codes to execute the analysis with covariates. Export Tables E.3, E.4 and Figures E.3, E.4 in the Online Appendix. 
* <code>analysis_g1_withpredv_nosatisficer_v2.R</code> Codes to execute the analysis with pretreatment trust in the JSDF as a covariate. Export Tables E.5, E.6 and Figures E.5, E.6 in the Online Appendix.
